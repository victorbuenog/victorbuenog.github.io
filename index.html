About

I'm a Mechanical Engineering PhD candidate at Santa Clara University specializing in fluid dynamics, thermal systems, and CFD modeling. My work spans microfluidic cooling for high-performance computing, computational fluid dynamics, and the integration of machine learning into engineering applications.

Outside of research I build software (web apps, data pipelines), lead student engineering organizations, and teach mechanical engineering as a graduate TA.
